Orion is a privately hosted, public-facing encyclopedia project established in 2026. Unlike specific technical documentation repositories, Orion is designed as a general knowledge base, structured similarly to traditional encyclopedias such as Wikipedia or Fandom.

History

The project was founded on February 8, 2026, by Shane, known online as Rhalza. The wiki was created to serve as a structured repository for information that extends beyond software development logs, encompassing a wide range of topics of interest to the creator.

Administration

The site is currently maintained by a single administrator:

  • Name: Shane
  • Alias: Rhalza
  • Age: 28
  • Role: Founder & Lead Editor

Technical Structure

Orion is built as a static site hosted on GitHub Pages. It utilizes a custom monochrome design language featuring cold slate and purple tones, prioritizing readability and a clean, rectangular user interface over modern rounded aesthetics.

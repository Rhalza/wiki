Orion (Wiki)

From Orion, the free encyclopedia

Orion is a personal wiki project and general knowledge repository created and maintained by Rhalza. Unlike traditional development logs or technical blogs, Orion aims to structure information in an encyclopedic format, providing a persistent and organized database for a wide variety of subjects.

The project is hosted on GitHub Pages and utilizes a custom static site structure, eschewing heavy content management systems in favor of raw HTML, CSS, and JavaScript for maximum control over layout and performance.

History and Purpose

Founded in 2023, the wiki was established to consolidate scattered notes, documentation, and research into a single, searchable platform. The name "Orion" references the constellation, symbolizing a collection of distinct points of light forming a recognizable whole.

Development

The infrastructure of Orion is built to mimic the visual language of major wiki farms (such as Fandom or Miraheze) while maintaining a distinct, dark-mode-first aesthetic known as the "Cold Slate" theme.

About the Creator

Rhalza (real name Shane) is the 28-year-old creator and administrator of the Orion project. He serves as the primary contributor and editor-in-chief of the content within the repository.
